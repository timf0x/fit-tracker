System A — Forge

Embossed metallic medallions with layered depth.
Inspired by military medals & luxury watchmaking.

Design Philosophy

  • Multi-ring construction: outer frame → decorative ring → inner emblem
  • Metallic SVG gradients per tier (bronze/silver/gold/platinum)
  • Embossed center icon with shadow depth
  • Tick marks like a chronograph bezel
  • Shine sweep animation on unlocked badges
  • Soft glow pulse matching tier color

System B — Crystal

Hexagonal gem-cut badges with prismatic light.
Inspired by gemstones & holographic materials.

Design Philosophy

  • Hexagonal shape — distinctive, game-achievement feel
  • Faceted inner surfaces with light refraction effect
  • Rotating orbital ring of small dots around unlocked badges
  • Prismatic shimmer animation on hover/unlock
  • Inner icon etched with depth shadow
  • Frosted glass effect for locked state

System C — Relic

Ancient artifact medallions with mystical energy.
Inspired by mythological seals & arcane symbols.

Design Philosophy

  • Circular medallion with ornate runic border ring
  • Inner icon rendered as an engraved seal/emblem
  • Slowly rotating outer rune ring on unlocked badges
  • Floating particle effect (energy wisps)
  • Deep emboss with inner glow for depth
  • Each tier has distinct energy color & intensity
