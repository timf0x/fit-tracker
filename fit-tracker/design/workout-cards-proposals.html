Trade-offs

A

Monochrome Glyph

Kill all color on icons. Neutral frosted glass box, white glyph at 60% opacity. Surgical clean.

+ Instant premium feel
+ Zero visual noise
+ Easiest to implement
- Cards look samey at a glance
- Loses quick category scanning
B

Accent Edge

Remove icons entirely. 3px vertical gradient strip carries the color. Typography-first, editorial layout.

+ Most unique / editorial
+ Room for extra metadata tags
+ Very clean hierarchy
- Biggest departure from current
- Cards feel "text-heavy"
C

Kinetic Orbs

Rotating conic-gradient ring around a dark circle with a faint icon. Color stays but feels alive, not flat.

+ Most "kinetic" / premium
+ Keeps color identity
+ Animated = feels alive
- Animation cost on long lists
- Slightly more complex impl
